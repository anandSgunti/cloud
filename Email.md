## Root Cause

We identified the cause of the performance degradation. The model was trained on well-lit office images, but it is now receiving darker, lower-clarity warehouse images. This shift in deployment conditions materially impacted accuracy and contributed to processing slowdowns that affected timelines.

## Immediate Actions

As soon as the variance was detected, we paused further rollout to limit broader impact and completed a focused root cause analysis. We have initiated retraining using targeted data augmentation to align the model with warehouse conditions and are already seeing early validation signals that performance is normalizing.

## Timeline

We are targeting full validation and redeployment within 2–3 weeks, with measurable performance benchmarks confirmed prior to release. In parallel, we are implementing environmental validation checks to ensure future deployments proactively account for input-condition shifts before rollout.
